Keyword Monitor

How It Works

Tired of manually checking websites for important updates? Let Keyword Monitor do the work for you!

This tool automatically tracks changes on any website you specify. Simply provide a URL and the keywords you're interested in, and we'll notify you via email or Telegram as soon as the keyword count increases.

A Real-World Example:

Imagine you're job hunting and waiting for a role to open up. You could monitor the Accenture careers page for keywords like "Backend Developer," "Spring Boot," and "Microservices." The moment a new position matching these terms is posted, you'll get an instant notification. No more constant manual checks!

Simple Steps to Get Started:
  1. Enter a URL: The website you want to track.
  2. Add Keywords: The specific terms you're looking for (e.g., "new arrivals," "job opening," "out of stock").
  3. Set Up Notifications: Provide your email or Telegram Chat ID.
  4. Relax: We'll let you know when something changes!
